MISSION STATEMENT
Mission

Uniting the community through the universal language of music and making performance opportunities more accessible for young musicians has been Bel Canto Initiative’s main vision ever since its launch in 2022. We believe music has the power to heal and connect people of all ages and backgrounds which is why we strive to bring joy to people’s days and help those in need through creative events/concerts.

OUR VALUES

With a focus in fostering an inclusive environment that reaches people of diverse ages and cultural backgrounds, we provide youth with the chance to connect with seniors through senior center concerts and engage with the community through general public performances.

Through our initiative, youth are able to hone and develop their musical and leadership skills by collaborating with driven and passionate members of the team while cultivating meaningful friendships along the way.

We aim to make innovative opportunities to help out our community through music more accessible to everyone. Our events allow youth to showcase their musical talents and express their artistic voices while simultaneously contributing to charitable causes.
